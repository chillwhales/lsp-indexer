## Decisions Made

- Used `IncludeResult<Nft>` directly for NFT sub-context (not `NftResult`) because collection/holder are absent in owned-token NFT context — custom `OwnedTokenNftScalarFieldMap` maps only the 8 available fields
- Used `IncludeResult<OwnedAsset>` directly for OwnedAsset sub-context (not `OwnedAssetResult`) because only 3 scalar fields (balance, block, timestamp) are available — custom `OwnedTokenOwnedAssetFieldMap`
- `OwnedTokenNftInclude` and `OwnedTokenOwnedAssetInclude` are structural subtypes of `NftInclude` and `OwnedAssetInclude` — passed safely to `parseNft` and `parseOwnedAsset` without type errors
