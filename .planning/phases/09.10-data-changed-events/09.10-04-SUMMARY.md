## Decisions Made

- **dataKeyName display pattern:** Bold resolved name when known (e.g., "LSP3Profile"), with raw hex key truncated to 20 chars in mono below. When null, shows "(Unknown Key)" in muted italic with raw hex below.
- **4-tab layout:** Combined both domains into single page with 4 tabs rather than separate pages — matches the dual-domain nature of this phase.
- **NFT info as field-presence guards:** NftInfoSection accepts `Record<string, unknown>` and extracts fields safely — no import from nfts domain types, consistent with plan requirement.
- **nft boolean in scalar includes:** Since nft is a boolean include (not sub-include object), it goes in the TOKEN_ID_DATA_CHANGED_EVENT_INCLUDE_FIELDS array directly.
