## Decisions Made

- **FollowerCard accepts `Record<string, unknown>`:** Consistent with other domain cards. `'key' in obj` guards determine which sections render. Typed narrowing enforced at hook consumer level, not card level.
- **Two SubIncludeSections per list tab:** `followerProfile` and `followedProfile` are independently controllable sub-include sections using `PROFILE_INCLUDE_FIELDS`. Each side of the relationship can have different profile fields fetched.
- **CountTab simplified UI:** No filters/sorts/includes — just address input and two large numbers (followerCount, followingCount). Consistent with a utility hook that returns simple aggregates.
- **IsFollowingTab simplified UI:** No filters/sorts/includes — just two address inputs and a YES/NO Badge result. Matches the boolean nature of the hook.
- **Independent address inputs per tab:** Each tab has its own address input. No shared state across tabs (per CONTEXT.md: "each tab has its own independent inputs").
